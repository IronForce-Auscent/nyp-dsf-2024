Singapore Healthy Cooking Competition 2016

Rules and Regulatioons

  • Competitors are given 20 minutes to shop for ingredients at NTUC next to PCK Community Center
  • Total price of ingredients should not be more than $30.
  • Each team is to prepare
    1. One pot of soup
    2. 2 dishes
  • Total time spent for preparation and cooking should not exceed an hour
  • Judges' decision is final.
